Yellow NSC Guide - February 3, 2017

設定

- 系統選項：Fast / Off / Shift - 取名：主角 - a，勁敵 - a

真新鎮 / Pallet Town

- 不拿傷藥 (Potion)，領取皮卡丘並命名為 a，之後與宿敵對戰

宿敵戰

- 電擊 (Thundershock) 到底 - 輸贏不重要，但等級高好闖關 - 離開研究所，前往 1 號道路

1 號道路

- 在常磐市 (Viridian City) 寶可夢中心恢復體力，此舉作用為設置傳送點 - 超商取貨 - 完成送貨事件後回到常磐市

常磐市 / Viridian City

- 前往超商購買精靈球 (Poke Ball) * 4 - 離開超商，前往常磐森林

常磐森林 / Viridian Forest

- 遇敵就逃跑 - 撿起地上的傷藥 - Manip a Deathfly on the 3rd Bug Catcher. - Video for the Deathfly Manip: Yellow NSC Deathfly Manip - Spam Tailwhip or Thundershock, if you won or lost the Rival Fight respectively.

2 號道路

- Head back up Route 2 and walk through the grass, catch any encounter if you happen to get one. - 再次進入森林

常磐森林 / Viridian Forest

- If you didn't catch a dummy pokemon yet, catch any encounter you see here, but try to avoid catching Metapods. - All trainers are deactivated, proceed through the forest.

尼比市 / Pewter City

- Enter the center and talk to the Jigglypuff from the right. - 往下 1 格，往右 6 格 - 往上 1 格 - Walk down and up 16 to 17 times, where each iteration is walking 2 steps down and 2 steps up. - Walk back to Pikachu and talk to him. - If you done this correctly, your in game timer should be set to 255:59. - Deposit Pikachu and leave the center. - Head to the Mart. - 購買離洞繩 (Escape Rope) * 1 - 前往尼比道館

尼比道館

- Walk into the line of sight of the one trainer in the Gym so that he walks to you (this is important). If he does not walk to you, the game will freeze and you will have to reset. - Simply die to the trainer as fast as possible.

常磐市 / Viridian City

- Do THE Manip: [Yellow NSC] THE Manip 1.0 - Walk to Viridian Forest but make sure you jump down a ledge on your way (this is also important). - If you done the manip correctly, a mart should pop up.

常磐森林 / Viridian Forest

- Select the Buy option and do the following: (Don't mash B, keizaron) > Press A on the 3rd item (Bicycle) so that you will asked to choose an amount, but press B to backout. > Buy 1 of the tmtrainer item in the 2nd slot. > Exit the mart shop entirely. - 使用離洞繩 (Escape Rope)

常磐市 / Viridian City

- Enter the Mart and do the following: > Walk 1 left and attempt to save, but press B at the Yes/No dialog. > Toss 116 from the glitched item in your 3rd slot (you currently have 186). > Walk 2 up and flash your item bag. > Talk to the seller. > Attempt to sell the glitched item. > Open the buy menu and attempt to buy the SS Ticket, but back out. > Buy the glitched item in the 2nd slot. > Close mart entirely. > Walk 2 down, and attempt to save again. > Toss 1 from the glitched item in slot 3 of your inventory. > Toss the first 2 items in your inventory (Pokeball and Potion). > Attempt to save again and walk 1 right.

ITEM UNDERFLOW

> Toss 253 of the glitched item in the FIRST slot. > Swap slot 1 with slot 2 twice. Each time, the two stacks will merge. > Scroll down past a load of []x[] items until you hit some "real" items. > Find a stack of []x[] x[]9 and select it to swap. > Swap it with Ultraball x0 4 slots below. > Toss 11 from the []x[] x[]9 stack (Note: Using the item will crash the game!). > Select it again and swap it with Masterball x1 4 slots below. > Close the menu, and exit the Mart. - If you done everything correctly, you should end up in the Hall of Fame! - Timing ends when it flashes white to the credits.
