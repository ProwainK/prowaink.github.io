[Yellow NSC] Non-255:59 Route

https://pastebin.com/NcEsAB6a

準備

- 標題畫面輸入 Up + B + Select 刪除存檔 - Soft Reset：A + B + Start + Select

[亂數控制] TID

- 幫主角取名選字時，一進去就 Reset - 到系統選項界面時都維持預先輸入狀態

設定

- 系統選項：Fast / Off / Shift - 取名：主角 - 預設名字 ASH， 勁敵 - (1) TAS (10m 17s) ×S×× (2) 舊套路 ×S× (× 是乘號，不是英文單字)

真新鎮

- 不拿傷藥 (Potion)，領取皮卡丘不用命名，之後與宿敵對戰

宿敵戰

- 電擊 (Thundershock) 到底 - 輸贏不重要，但等級高好闖關 - 離開研究所，前往 1 號道路

1 號道路

- 豆知識 (?)：按住 B 回頭走路不會撞到皮卡丘 - 遇敵就逃跑 - 超商取貨 - 繞到大木博士背後對話 - 完成送貨事件後回到常磐市

常磐便利商店

- 購買精靈球 (Poke Ball) * 5+ (至少一顆球用來抓寶可夢) - 離開超商，回頭到 1 號道路

1 號道路

- 抓一隻波波 (Pidgey) 或小拉達 (Rattata) - NOTE: Corner bonk between the ledge and Pikachu for style points - 捕捉 Lv 2 ~ 4 波波：遇到馬上丟球 小拉達、Lv 5 ~ 7 波波：抓之前來一發電擊 - 命名 Lv 4 小拉達：不用命名 波波、Lv 2/3 小拉達：取名為 A - 抓到這隻將用於 Deathfly Battle Manip

常磐寶可夢中心

- 寄放皮卡丘到電腦系統，然後恢復體力 (設置傳送點) - 看完老人教學後，前往 2 號道路

[亂數控制] 森林不遇敵

- 影片：https://youtu.be/KDRDvVE3VN4 - 54/60 機率不會遇敵 - 撿起地上的傷藥 (結尾巫術需要，手持 3 種以上道具物品) - NOTE: Make sure not to load final Bug Catcher before deathfly manip

Deathfly Manip

- 影片：https://youtu.be/DoPt1pxHPTo - 47/60 機率遇到 L9 Pidgeotto，11/60 機率遇到 L8 Pidgey (OHKO rate depends on your poke) - Buffer down in move menu with all Pidgeys (works even without a 2nd move) - Buffer select in move menu with all Rattatas - 沒有在一回合內掛掉的話，狂放招式直到結束

THE Manip

- 影片：https://youtu.be/DonALfmjZOk - 27/60 for NPC movement up to Town Map, hitting one of the first 4 textbox frames is ~88% for bird to move left - Stop movement for ~6 frames to allow bird to start moving left, then hold right - Adjacent frames: http://pastebin.com/raw/qBT86ks0 (listed early to late, each is a 2 frame window; target = FE)

森林便利商店

- 購買 TMTRAINER (巫術道具，在清單第二個) * 1，然後離開超商 - 預先輸入「右」，往右一格再往下走 - 回到常磐便利商店

常磐便利商店

- Upon entering, walk 1 left and "attempt" to save (press B at the Yes/No dialog) - Toss TMTRAINER x116 (you currently have 186, so mash down to get to 116) - Walk 2 up and flash your item bag - Open shop and attempt to sell TMTRAINER ("I can't put a price on that.") - Buy 1 more TMTRAINER (2nd in buy menu), then close the shop - Walk 2 down and "attempt" to save again - Toss TMTRAINER x1, then toss your top 2 items (Poke Ball, Potion) - "Attempt" to save again and walk 2 right

Underflow

- 影片：https://youtu.be/ofcFqkG3BQg - Toss TMTRAINER x253 from the *first* slot - Swap slot 1 with slot 2 twice (causes the stacks to merge) - Before scrolling down, "attempt" to use TMTRAINER (not necessary, but avoids 1 click item) - Scroll down past a load of []x[] items until you hit some "real" items. - Swap TM41 x[]6 with TM41 x80 (rival name) - Swap TM41 x99 with item below Thunderbadge (map script ptr) - Swap Thunderbadge (map text ptr) with Helix Fossil (map id) - 關閉選單 1 error. - Timing ends when it flashes white to the credits.
